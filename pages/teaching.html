__ Teaching Experiences _________

I teach across nursing, health research, harm reduction, and 2S/LGBTQ+ health, drawing on both my clinical background and my research in health equity. My teaching philosophy is grounded in compassion, structural competency, and intersectional thinking, and I work to make learning accessible, engaging, and relevant to real-world practice. I aim to empower students to think critically about health systems, challenge inequities, and approach care with curiosity and cultural humility.
